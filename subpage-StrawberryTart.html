Strawberry Pretzel Tart

Difficulty: Easy !

How to prepare


  1. Ground the pretzels into a fine powder using a food processor. Measure to ensure you have 1/2 cup, then return to the food processor along with the flour, sugar, and salt. Pulse until well distributed.

  2. Add the butter, shortening, and egg and pulse until it forms a thick dough with large crumbly chunks.

  3. Turn it out onto a sheet of plastic wrap and form it into a disk. Wrap and chill for 1 hour.

    4. PRETZEL CRUMBLE


  4. While that chills, making the pretzel crumble topping. Preheat the oven to 300F and line a medium-sized baking sheet with parchment paper.

  5. Combine all of the ingredients in a bowl and then spread out in an even layer on the baking sheet.

  6. Bake for 15 minutes, tossing halfway. 

  7. Transfer to a bowl to cool.

    8. STRAWBERRY TOPPING


  8. Add the strawberries and sugar to a bowl and toss to combine. Let them sit out at room temperature to macerate.

    9. PRETZEL PIE CRUST


  9. Back to the crust. Take it out of the refrigerator and leave it out for about 15 minutes to soften slightly. Then roll it out into a circle about 10 inches in diameter to fit a 9 inch tart pan. If may crack and crumble which is OK. Carefully transfer the crust to the tart pan. Again, if it breaks apart just mend it back together inside the a tart pan.

  10. Press it tight and then freeze for 10 minutes while you preheat the oven to 350F.
  11. Once chilled, tear off a sheet of parchment paper that’s large enough to cover the tart. Scrunch it up into a ball and then unwrap it. This helps loosen it up so you can fit it inside the pie shell. Fill with pie weights (or dried beans) and bake for 20 minutes.

  12. Remove the pie weights and bake for an additional 10 minutes.

  13. While the pie shell cools, make the cream cheese filling.

    14. CREAM CHEESE FILLING


  14. In a bowl using a hand or stand mixer with the whisk attachment, mix together the softened cream cheese and sugar. Once it’s smooth and well combined, add the heavy cream and vanilla.

  15. Crank it up to medium-high speed and whip for about 3-5 minutes or until it becomes smooth and fluffy.

  16. Spread the filling inside the cooled pie shell.

  17. Allow any juices to drain off the strawberries and lay them on top of the cream cheese filling. 

  18. Mix the jam with about 2 teaspoons of water and brush it on top of the strawberries. Heat it up in the microwave for about 15-20 seconds if it’s still too thick to brush.

  19. Chill the tart for 2 hours to set. To serve, top with a layer of the pretzel crunch. Enjoy!

Ingredients


Quantity Item Name
½ cup (60g) ground pretzels
¾ cup (95g) all purpose flour
2 teaspoon granulated sugar
½ teaspoon salt
6 tablespoon unsalted butter, cold and cubed
2 tablespoon vegetable shortening
1 large egg, chilled

Pretzel Crunch
1 cup (110g) crushed pretzels
3 tablespoon unsalted butter
2 tablespoon light brown sugar
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
2 tablespoon all purpose flour

Cream Cheese Filling
226.80 grams full fat cream cheese, room temp
¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
½ cup (135ml) heavy whipping cream, chilled
½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Strawberry Topping
500 grams fresh strawberries, cleaned and sliced
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
¼ cup strawberry jam
