Forest Moss Cake

Difficulty: Easy !

How to prepare


    FOREST MOSS CAKE


  1. All ingredients should be at room temperature.

  2. Place the frozen leaf spinach in a sieve over a bowl. Leave to defrost, then squeeze it against the sieve to get rid of the moisture (puree in blender, if needed). Fresh baby spinach – puree in blender or food processor.

  3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar together until pale, thick and fluffy. Gradually add the oil and beat to combine. Add the spinach puree, lemon juice and zest, and stir to combine. Add the sifted flour with baking powder and mix with a spatula just to combine.

  4. Prepare a baking pan with a diameter of 23 cm lined with baking paper. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan and bake at 165ºC for 40 to 45 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Remove from the oven, cool.

    5. MASCORPONE CREAM


  5. All ingredients should be chilled.

  6. Place all ingredients together in a large bowl and whisk until stiff.

    7. TO ASSEMBLE:


  7. Level the cake – gently cut the top off the cake. Crumble the top into a separate bowl and set aside.

  8. Cut the cake horizontally through the centre of the cake to make two layers. Sandwich the layers of the cake with mascarpone cream, leaving 3 Tbsp cream for the top. Spread the rest of the cream over the entire cake and sprinkle with crumbs.

  9. Decorate with the pomegranate seeds and blueberries. Chill in the fridge until ready to use.

  10. Store in the refrigerator.

Ingredients


Quantity Item Name
400 g fresh baby spinach or frozen leaf spinach
3 large eggs
¾ – 1 cup caster sugar
¾ cup sunflower or rapeseed oil
2 cups plain flour
2 ½ teaspoon baking powder
2 tablespoon lemon juice
1 lemon zest

Mascorpone cream
250 ml whipping cream
125 g mascarpone cheese
1.5 tablespoon icing sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla or lemon extract or lemon juice
